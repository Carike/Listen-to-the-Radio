Listen to the radio

When Don Williams sang "Listen to the radio", I doubt that this is what he had in mind

With the advancements of technology, people can broadcast live from their cell phones and one such a platform is Mixlr. They enable creators to have their own virtual station so to speak.

So as a proof of concept I'm creating a mini website to display the audio player and adding the Twitter feed for viewers to engage with the brand using social media.
